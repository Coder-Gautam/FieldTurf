FAQ

Good Questions. Straight
Answers.

Can I buy turf directly from FieldTurf Landscape? +
Yes. If you build, quote, or install outdoor spaces, this page is for you. Contractors, turf installers, outdoor building pros, hardscape contractors, outdoor living pros, lawn care companies adding turf services, and anyone else who builds outdoor spaces professionally is welcome to reach out about buying turf products.
Do I need to become a dealer first? +
No. You can start by talking with our team about products for a current job or future projects. No contract required to start the conversation or buy turf.
Can I buy turf for one project? +
Yes. If you have a current project, tell us what you are working on and our team will help you understand your product options and next steps.
Do you work with contractors who are new to turf? +
Yes. We support experienced turf installers as well as contractors adding turf to their services for the first time. We can help you choose the right product regardless of where you are in your turf business.
Can you help me choose the right turf? +
Yes. Our team can match products to the application, including yards, pet areas, playgrounds, putting greens, commercial spaces, rooftops, patios, and specialty projects.
Do you provide training or product guidance? +
Yes. Contractors can access product guidance, application support, sales tools, and training resources to help quote and sell with more confidence.
Can I grow into a larger FieldTurf relationship later? +
Yes. Some contractors start with individual projects and grow into a larger FieldTurf Landscape relationship over time, including access to additional support, marketing resources, and lead opportunities where available.
Are territory opportunities available? +
Territory opportunities may be available for contractors who grow into a larger relationship with FieldTurf Landscape. They are not required to start buying turf or to have any conversation with us.
What happens after I contact FieldTurf? +
Our team will follow up about your project, product needs, timing, and service area. From there, we help you find the right product and next step. No pressure, no contract required.
